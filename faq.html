Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using TAIW Smart Auto-Apply.

What is TAIW Smart Auto-Apply?

It is an intelligent tool that analyzes job requirements and your profile to generate personalized, high-quality application text in seconds. The AI understands the difference between reasoning tasks, RLHF, data quality work, and more.

Is TAIW free to use?

Yes. You can browse opportunities and submit a limited number of applications for free. TAIW Pro ($19/mo or $149/yr) unlocks unlimited Smart Auto-Apply, priority job access, and advanced analytics.

How good is the generated application text?

Extremely good. Our models are fine-tuned on thousands of successful AI trainer applications. You can always edit the output before submitting. Most trainers report significantly higher response rates.

Which platforms do you support?

Currently Outlier, Scale AI, Surge AI, and Micro1 Talent. We are rapidly adding more platforms. Pro users get early access to new integrations.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption. Your profile data and generated applications are only visible to you. We never share your information with platforms without your explicit action.

Can I use TAIW on mobile?

Yes. The entire experience (including Smart Auto-Apply) is fully responsive and works beautifully on phones and tablets.
