TAIW (TrainAIWorld) is building the infrastructure that connects talented individuals with frontier AI labs through intelligent matching and Smart Auto-Apply technology.

Our Mission

Make high-quality AI training work accessible, efficient, and rewarding — especially for trainers in emerging markets across Southeast Asia, Latin America, and Africa.

The Problem We Solve

AI trainers spend hours writing repetitive applications. Quality varies. Many talented people are overlooked. TAIW fixes this with intelligent tools that level the playing field.

Our Impact

Over 19,400 trainers placed. Average hourly rate of $38. Trainers in 87 countries are now earning sustainably through better access to opportunities.

How We Think About AI Training

We believe that the people who help train frontier models deserve the best tools, fair compensation, and transparent processes. TAIW is not just a job board — it is infrastructure for the human side of AI development.

Our Smart Auto-Apply technology understands the nuances of different task types (reasoning, RLHF, data quality, multimodal) and helps trainers present their strengths effectively.
